About Cozy Glory Shed

Discover our story, values, and what makes us special

Our Story

From humble beginnings to a premium farm stay destination

Nestled in the serene countryside, Cozy Glory Shed was born from a simple vision: to create a peaceful retreat where guests can reconnect with nature without sacrificing comfort. What started as a family farm has blossomed into a beloved destination for those seeking tranquility and a break from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Our journey began in 2020 when we first opened our doors to guests looking for an authentic farm experience. Over the years, we've carefully developed our property to offer the perfect blend of rustic charm and modern amenities, always staying true to our roots and commitment to sustainable tourism.

Today, Cozy Glory Shed stands as a testament to our passion for hospitality and love for the land. We take pride in offering a unique experience that combines the simplicity of farm life with the luxury of a premium retreat.

Our Values

Hospitality

Community
